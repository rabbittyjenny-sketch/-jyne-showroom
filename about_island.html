WHO IS JYNE

Jyne (Jenjira Jayasin)
SELF-STARTER · STANDARDS-DRIVEN · QUIETLY CONFIDENT

Jyne didn't plan this career. She saw an opportunity — and moved.

Her foundation starts somewhere most people don't expect. She graduated with a degree in Industrial Computer Technology — at a time when there were no ready-made programs, no drag-and-drop tools, no AI. She wrote code in Pascal and C+, built hardware from scratch, and etched circuit boards by hand. She learned that systems don't build themselves. Someone has to understand them deeply enough to make them work. That person has always been her.

From the day she graduated, she was selling — across jewelry, hospitality, and every room she walked into. Reading people, earning trust, managing relationships from security staff to C-suite to international VVIP guests. The environment changed. The instinct didn't.

For close to a decade, she worked inside two of Bangkok's most internationally recognized fine dining operations — supporting and running the business side for Chef Chumpol Jangprai (R-HAAN, 2 Michelin Stars, Iron Chef Thailand) and Chef Andy Yangeksakul (Table 38 Bangkok; founder of Rhong Tiam, New York — the first Thai-operated restaurant to earn a Michelin Star in the US). In environments where anonymous global inspectors scrutinize every single detail, she learned what real operational excellence looks like — not in theory, but under sustained pressure.

Three years ago, she saw live commerce the way she'd always seen things: this can work. She became a freelance Live Commerce Host across TikTok LIVE, Facebook Live, and Shopee Live. No team. No blueprint. No one to define the standard for her. So she built her own — scripting, systems, platform logic, technical setup — solo.

The result: 6-figure GMV in a regular 2-hour live session. Repeatedly.
When people see the output, the first reaction is usually: "That looks easy."
That reaction is the point. Things that are built well look effortless. They aren't.

"Anyone can use AI. But using it and knowing how to use it — the results are very different."

"Three years ago, live streaming wasn't in any plan. But I saw where it was going. Everyone can go live — what got me to 6-figure sessions wasn't talent. It was doing the homework alone, holding myself to a standard nobody taught me. People see the result and assume it's simple. The numbers speak."

"Don't try to make it perfect. Make it work."
